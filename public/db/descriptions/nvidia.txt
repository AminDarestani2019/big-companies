Nvidia is a global leader in graphics processing units (GPUs) and AI computing. Founded in 1993, it initially focused on gaming hardware but has since expanded into high-performance computing, data centers, and AI research. The company's GPU technology powers everything from gaming consoles to deep learning models. Nvidia's CUDA platform has become essential in scientific and AI applications. In recent years, it has made strategic acquisitions in AI and chip design. Its products are widely used by developers, gamers, and data scientists. Nvidia continues to push the boundaries of real-time rendering and machine learning. The company plays a pivotal role in autonomous vehicles and edge computing. Its innovative culture has earned it a leading reputation in Silicon Valley.
