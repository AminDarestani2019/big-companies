AngelList is an online platform launched in 2010 to connect startups with investors. It was co-founded by Naval Ravikant and Babak Nivi. Initially designed as an email list, it evolved into a full-featured investment platform. AngelList allows startups to raise funds via syndicates, simplifying venture capital for both founders and backers. It introduced rolling funds, democratizing access to venture investing. The platform also hosts startup job listings and offers talent-matching services. AngelList has helped fund thousands of early-stage companies. It operates AngelList Venture, AngelList Talent, and Product Hunt as part of its ecosystem. The company has become a cornerstone of the global startup infrastructure. It continues to innovate in startup funding and hiring.
