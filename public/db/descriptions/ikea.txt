IKEA is a global furniture and home goods retailer founded in Sweden in 1943. It’s known for flat-pack furniture, modern design, and affordable pricing. IKEA stores offer immersive showroom experiences and food courts. Its self-assembly model reduces costs and enhances scalability. The company emphasizes sustainability and uses renewable materials. IKEA operates in over 50 countries with massive warehouse-style stores. It also invests in smart home and digital transformation. IKEA’s catalog and minimalist style became cultural icons. Its supply chain is among the most efficient in retail. IKEA continues to lead in affordable and functional home design.
