Bungie is a renowned American video game developer established in 1991. It initially gained fame with the Marathon series and later revolutionized console gaming with the Halo franchise, developed for Xbox. Bungie split from Microsoft in 2007 to become an independent company again. Its next flagship title, Destiny, introduced a new model of shared-world shooter with online multiplayer. Bungie is known for rich storytelling, deep lore, and player communities. The company focuses on building games as a service, continually updating content. Bungie emphasizes player feedback and community-driven development. It has been a pioneer in blending FPS and MMO elements. In 2022, it was acquired by Sony Interactive Entertainment but retains creative independence.
