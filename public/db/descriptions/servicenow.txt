ServiceNow is a cloud-based software company specializing in digital workflow automation. It was founded in 2004 and initially focused on IT service management (ITSM), but has since expanded to offer enterprise solutions across IT operations, HR, customer service, security, and more. ServiceNow’s Now Platform enables organizations to create custom applications that automate and streamline complex workflows. The platform uses machine learning and predictive analytics to enhance productivity and incident resolution. With an emphasis on low-code development, it empowers both technical and non-technical users to build solutions. Its extensive integration capabilities make it ideal for large-scale digital transformation. ServiceNow is used by many Fortune 500 companies and government agencies. It’s widely regarded as a leader in ITSM and enterprise service management (ESM).
