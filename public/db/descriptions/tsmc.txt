Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) is the world’s largest dedicated independent semiconductor foundry. Founded in 1987, it manufactures chips designed by companies like Apple, AMD, and Nvidia. TSMC’s advanced manufacturing technologies set industry standards. It operates fabs that produce 5nm and even 3nm chips. The company is critical to the global tech supply chain. It is headquartered in Hsinchu, Taiwan. TSMC invests heavily in R&D and capital infrastructure. It plays a central role in the AI, smartphone, and computing industries. TSMC’s market position is seen as geopolitically strategic. Its reliability and scale make it a backbone of modern electronics.
