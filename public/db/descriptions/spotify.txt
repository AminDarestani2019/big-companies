Spotify is a global music streaming platform that revolutionized how people consume audio content. Launched in 2008 in Sweden, Spotify offers access to millions of songs, podcasts, and audiobooks through a freemium model, combining free ad-supported streaming with premium subscriptions. The company pioneered algorithmic recommendations, creating personalized playlists like Discover Weekly and Release Radar. Spotify also empowers artists and podcasters by providing insights into their audience and performance metrics. With mobile, desktop, and smart speaker compatibility, it ensures seamless user experiences across devices. Spotify's acquisitions, like Anchor and Megaphone, have expanded its influence in the podcasting space. It continues to innovate in audio discovery and monetization. Spotify remains at the forefront of music streaming, shaping the future of digital entertainment globally.
