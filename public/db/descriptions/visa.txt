Visa is a global payments technology company that facilitates electronic funds transfers. Founded in 1958, it operates one of the world’s largest payment networks. Visa does not issue cards directly but partners with financial institutions. It processes billions of transactions annually across more than 200 countries. The company is a pioneer in digital payments and contactless technologies. Visa has expanded into mobile and cryptocurrency-related innovations. Its secure, scalable infrastructure supports commerce worldwide. VisaNet, the company’s core processing network, is renowned for reliability. It actively combats fraud with AI and tokenization. Visa plays a foundational role in the global financial ecosystem.
