Epic Systems is a leading U.S. healthcare software company founded in 1979. It specializes in electronic health records (EHR) and hospital management solutions. Epic’s software is used by major hospitals and healthcare networks globally. It offers integrated tools for scheduling, billing, and patient care. The company is privately held and maintains a unique corporate culture. Epic places strong emphasis on data privacy and interoperability. It uses a campus-style headquarters in Wisconsin. The system supports telehealth and patient portals. Epic’s influence has shaped the digitization of healthcare. It continuously innovates to meet evolving medical needs.
