Emergence Capital is a leading venture capital firm based in Menlo Park, California. It was founded in 2003 with a focus on early-stage enterprise cloud startups. Emergence has invested in iconic companies such as Salesforce, Zoom, and Veeva Systems. The firm coined the term "coaching networks" to describe next-gen enterprise platforms. It emphasizes business models with strong recurring revenue and network effects. Emergence partners take a hands-on approach to mentoring founders. The firm operates with a tight-knit team and a long-term vision. Its investments often target SaaS companies with high growth potential. Emergence has won numerous accolades in the VC industry. It continues to shape the future of work and enterprise software.
