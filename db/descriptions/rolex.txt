Rolex is a Swiss luxury watchmaker founded in 1905. It is synonymous with precision, innovation, and timeless elegance. Rolex developed the first waterproof watch and automatic date-changing mechanism. Its watches are status symbols and collectibles. The company maintains high vertical integration, producing in-house movements and components. Rolex is a key player in sports sponsorship and exploration. Its marketing emphasizes excellence, heritage, and achievement. The brand is renowned for its durability and craftsmanship. Rolex watches often appreciate in value. The company is privately owned by the Hans Wilsdorf Foundation.
