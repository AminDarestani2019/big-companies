Duolingo is a widely popular language-learning platform founded in 2011 by Luis von Ahn and Severin Hacker. It offers gamified lessons across dozens of languages, making language acquisition accessible and engaging. The app uses bite-sized lessons with listening, reading, writing, and speaking exercises. Its freemium model allows users to access most features for free, with optional paid upgrades for enhanced functionality. Duolingo uses AI and data analytics to personalize learning paths. The company has expanded into literacy and mathematics education. With over 500 million users, it has become a leader in edtech. Duolingo has received praise for democratizing education worldwide. It went public in 2021, further boosting its market presence. The owl mascot "Duo" has become an internet icon.
