Twitch is a live streaming platform primarily used for gaming, launched in 2011 and acquired by Amazon in 2014. It allows users to broadcast their gameplay and interact with viewers in real time. The platform became a hub for esports, music broadcasts, and creative content. Twitch’s influence on gaming culture is massive, giving rise to new careers and communities. It supports monetization through subscriptions, ads, and donations. Twitch also serves as a social network where streamers build strong fan bases. Its real-time chat function enhances engagement. With millions of daily viewers, it dominates the game streaming industry. It’s also a cultural driver, influencing meme culture and internet trends. Twitch continues to evolve with new features and broader content.
