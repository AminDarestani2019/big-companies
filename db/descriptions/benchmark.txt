Benchmark Capital is a Silicon Valley-based venture capital firm founded in 1995. It is known for its lean partnership model and selective investment approach. Benchmark was an early investor in companies like eBay, Twitter, Uber, and Instagram. The firm typically invests in seed and early-stage startups in software, internet, and mobile sectors. It focuses on creating long-term value rather than rapid exits. Benchmark partners work closely with founders, often taking board seats. The firm avoids raising oversized funds to maintain agility. Benchmark is respected for its discipline and high-impact investments. Its portfolio includes several billion-dollar exits. The firm remains highly influential in the global VC ecosystem.
