The Savannah Bananas are a unique minor league baseball team known for their high-energy entertainment and unorthodox approach to the sport. Based in Savannah, Georgia, they play at Grayson Stadium and participate in both exhibition games and their own competitive league called Banana Ball. Founded in 2016, the team focuses on fan experience over traditional gameplay, integrating dancing players, comedic skits, and audience participation into every event. They’ve gained viral popularity on social media platforms like TikTok and YouTube. The Bananas have a loyal fanbase and often sell out games. Their business model is based on “fans first, entertainment always,” redefining what it means to watch baseball. By blending sports and performance art, the Savannah Bananas have become a cultural phenomenon.
