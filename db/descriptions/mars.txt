Mars Inc. is a global manufacturer of confectionery, pet food, and food products. Founded in 1911, it owns brands like M&M's, Snickers, Pedigree, and Royal Canin. Mars is a family-owned business with diverse revenue streams. The company invests in research for pet health and nutrition. Mars also engages in sustainability and ethical sourcing initiatives. It operates across more than 80 countries. Mars maintains a private and low-profile image. It emphasizes long-term thinking and employee ownership. The company is a major player in both food and veterinary care. Its legacy blends fun, flavor, and responsibility.
